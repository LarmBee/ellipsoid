Who are we ?

  • About Us

Ellipsoid Consultants Ltd is a multidisciplinary firm specializing in GIS & Mapping, Engineering Surveying, Civil Construction, and Environmental Consultancy. With a strong presence in Kenya and Tanzania, we are dedicated to delivering high-precision solutions that drive sustainable development and infrastructure growth.

  • Our Expertise

With a team of highly qualified Land and Engineering Surveyors, Civil Engineers, Environmental Experts, and GIS Specialists, we provide industry-leading solutions for:

  • Infrastructure Development – Roads, bridges, and commercial buildings
  • Renewable Energy Projects – Surveying and site planning for solar farms
  • GIS & Mapping Solutions – Advanced geospatial data for urban planning
  • Environmental Sustainability – Environmental Impact Assessments and audits
  • Our Mission

To leverage technology and expertise in GIS, surveying, and engineering to create impactful, long-term solutions for our clients.

  • Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative engineering and geospatial solutions for Africa’s sustainable future.
